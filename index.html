How are you feeling today? Sometimes do you feel like you are not right or you are doing enough of your life? That is totally ok, we all feel that!

I know Iam not a famous guy, or someone who is well known for things that I have achieved. You might be thinking, who is this guy to say that is all right to feel down sometimes, or who is he to say that we all can achieve what we want. Why should we listen to him?

To be honest, not even I know what Iam doing. I'm still figuring it out. What I want to be, carrer I want to persue. I tend to doubt some of my decisions. Am I going to be a good web developer? Why Am I doing this?
Well, lately I have been working on my personal development and it got me thinking the same about those guys. Who are they to say we can all be what we want to be?

I guess the first thing that changed in me was my way of thinking. If not even I believe in myself then who is going to? Lately I have been feeling much better about myself, about my studies, feeling like I can achieve so much more in my life than I ever thought.

Changing the way we see ourselves also changes the way we think about everything. It is not an easy path, I know I have been there. Decided to go overseas when I was 19, so believe me when I say I doubted myself. But everything is about what is going on in our heads, if we feed negative thoughts, if we do not believe and love ourselves. We have first change our cycles of thoughts. Try and make them positive. Changing is never easy, but we can do difficult stuff.

I realized that as soon as we start believe in ourselfes we feel more confident, and them people start seeing us this way too, many say we have to change our world within so them we can change the outta world. That was said by many best-seller writers as we can see in following quotes.

Quote

“Attitude is a choice. Happiness is a choice. Optimism is a choice. Kindness is a choice. Giving is a choice. Respect is a choice. Whatever choice you make makes you. Choose wisely.”

Roy T. Bennett, The Light in the Heart

Quote

“There are only two ways to live your life. One is as though nothing is a miracle. The other is as though everything is a miracle.”

Albert Einstein

Quote

“Fairy tales are more than true: not because they tell us that dragons exist, but because they tell us that dragons can be beaten.”

Neil Gaiman, Coraline

Quote

“You have brains in your head. You have feet in your shoes. You can steer yourself any direction you choose. You're on your own. And you know what you know. And YOU are the one who'll decide where to go...”

Dr. Seuss, Oh, the Places You'll Go!

Believe in yourself, that is the first step for the better life we all want!
